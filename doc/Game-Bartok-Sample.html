r7 :: Rule Source #

When a seven is played, the player must say 'have a nice day'. The following player must either play another seven, saying 'have a very nice day' or say 'thank you' and draw 2 cards. If futher sevens are played (each such player must add another very), the first player who cannot play one must say 'thank you very{n} much' and draw 2n cards.
